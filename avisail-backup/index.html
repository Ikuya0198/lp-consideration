Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact each business division?
Please use our contact form and specify which division you're interested in (Trading, Investment, or Technology). We'll connect you with the appropriate team within 24 hours.
Do you accept equity investments?
We generally do not accept equity investments. Instead, we offer project-based participation schemes that align with specific business initiatives. Please contact us for details about current opportunities.
Can I see case studies or examples of ADCS deployments?
Yes! Visit our Technology page to access sample security reports and learn more about our ADCS (Active Defense Cyber Security) solution.
What is the typical process for vessel trading transactions?
Our process includes: (1) Requirement consultation, (2) AI-matched inventory presentation, (3) Inspection arrangement, (4) Escrow/payment structure setup, (5) Registration support. The entire process typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on vessel type and location.
Which countries and regions do you operate in?
We operate globally with primary focus on Asia-Pacific, Middle East, and Europe. Our legal structure includes entities in Japan and Panama, with banking infrastructure in Estonia and Dubai.
