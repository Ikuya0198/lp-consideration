Vessel Specializations

We specialize in three high-demand vessel categories serving regional markets across Asia, the Middle East, and emerging economies.

Coasters (500-5000 DWT)

Regional Cargo Transport: Short-sea shipping vessels ideal for coastal and inter-island trade routes.

Primary Markets: Philippines, Indonesia, Middle East, West Africa

Typical Uses: General cargo, bulk commodities, regional supply chains

Source Regions: Japan (aging fleet replacement), Korea (new builds), Eastern Europe (refurbished vessels)

Harbor & Offshore Operations: Essential for port operations, towing, and offshore support.

Primary Markets: UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Philippines

Typical Uses: Ship assist, barge towing, offshore platform support

Source Regions: Japan (high-quality used units), Korea (modern builds), Netherlands (specialized offshore tugs)

Shallow-Water Logistics: Specialized vessels for remote areas without port infrastructure.

Primary Markets: Philippines, Indonesia, Pacific Islands, Myanmar

Typical Uses: Construction materials, heavy equipment, disaster relief, island supply

Source Regions: Japan (government surplus), Korea (commercial builds), Singapore (ex-military conversions)

Innovative Payment Structures

We offer flexible financing options designed to reduce buyer risk and accelerate transaction completion.

Escrow-Based Transactions

Secure Milestone Payments: Funds held by neutral third-party escrow agents (law firms, banks) and released based on predefined milestones: initial deposit on contract signing, inspection payment upon survey completion, balance transfer upon delivery and title registration.

Risk Mitigation: Protects buyers from seller default or misrepresentation. Ensures sellers receive payment upon fulfilling contractual obligations. Reduces counterparty risk in cross-border transactions.

ETF-Backed Financing

Innovative Collateral Structure: Buyers pledge ETF portfolios as collateral instead of traditional bank loans. Lower interest rates compared to shipfinance due to liquid collateral. No impact on corporate credit lines or balance sheet leverage.

Process: Client maintains ETF portfolio with our investment division. Portfolio value underwrites vessel purchase (typically 70-80% LTV). Flexible repayment structures tied to vessel cash flows.
