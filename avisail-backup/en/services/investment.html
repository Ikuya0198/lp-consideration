Investment Strategy

Our core approach focuses on high-yield carry trades combined with strategic ETF allocation, designed for high-net-worth individuals seeking superior risk-adjusted returns.

FX Carry Trade Automation

Interest Rate Differential Capture: We systematically exploit interest rate differentials across currency pairs, using AI to identify optimal entry points and manage exposure dynamically.

Target Markets: Focus on high-yielding currencies (TRY, MXN, BRL, ZAR) against low-rate funding currencies (JPY, CHF, EUR), with continuous monitoring of central bank policy shifts.

Risk Controls: Automated position sizing based on volatility regimes, correlation monitoring to prevent concentration, and rapid reallocation during risk-off environments.

ETF Portfolio Strategy

Diversified Exposure: Strategic allocation across equity indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ, emerging markets), fixed income (government bonds, corporate bonds), commodities (gold, energy), and alternative assets.

Dynamic Rebalancing: Aviscore continuously evaluates ETF scores based on factor exposures, valuations, and momentum indicators to optimize portfolio composition.

Tax Efficiency: ETF structure provides cost-effective diversification with favorable tax treatment compared to individual securities or mutual funds.

Why Aviscore
