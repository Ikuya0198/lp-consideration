ADCS: Active Defense Cyber Security

Automated Security Assessment Platform

ADCS is our flagship cybersecurity solution designed specifically for small-to-medium enterprises and municipal governments. Unlike traditional penetration testing that requires manual engagement, ADCS provides continuous, automated security assessments through AI-powered OSINT analysis.

View ADCS Product Overview See Sample Reports

Multi-Vector Scanning

10+ Parallel Security Checks:

DNS resolution & validation, SSL/TLS certificate inspection, Port enumeration & service detection, Web security headers analysis, WHOIS & domain registration, ASN & geolocation mapping, CMS & technology fingerprinting, Email provider identification, Subdomain discovery, Complete OSINT pipeline integration

OSINT Integration

9 Intelligence Sources:

Why ADCS

Cost-Effective

Traditional pentests cost $5,000-$50,000 per engagement. ADCS provides continuous monitoring at a fraction of the cost through automation, making enterprise-grade security accessible to SMEs and municipalities with limited budgets.

Continuous Assessment

Unlike annual pentests that provide point-in-time snapshots, ADCS enables monthly or quarterly scans to track security posture evolution, detect new vulnerabilities, and measure remediation progress over time.

Implementation-First

We use ADCS internally for our own infrastructure before offering it to clients. This ensures real-world validation, practical value, and continuous improvement based on operational experience rather than theoretical design.

Partner-Ready

White-label deployment model enables IT consultancies, MSPs, and system integrators to offer ADCS under their own brand. High margins and low operational overhead make it attractive for channel partners globally.
